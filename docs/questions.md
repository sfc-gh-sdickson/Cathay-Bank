# Cathay Bank Intelligence Agent - Test Questions

30+ complex test questions organized by category to validate the agent's capabilities across all tools.

---

## Customer Analytics (Cortex Analyst)

1. What is our total customer count and how are they distributed across customer segments?
2. What is the average credit score by customer segment, and which segment has the highest average income?
3. How many customers have been with Cathay Bank for more than 20 years? What is their average deposit balance?
4. Show me the top 10 customers by total relationship value (deposits plus loans).
5. What is the distribution of preferred languages among our active customers?
6. How many customers have a KYC status of 'Expired' and what are their risk ratings?
7. What percentage of our customers are in the 'Private Banking' segment and what is their average annual income?

## Deposit and Account Analysis (Cortex Analyst)

8. What is our total deposit base and how is it distributed across account types (Checking, Savings, Money Market, CD)?
9. What is the average account balance by branch state? Which state has the highest total deposits?
10. How many accounts are currently dormant and what is their total balance?
11. Show me the top 5 branches by total deposit volume and number of unique customers.
12. What is the month-over-month trend of new accounts opened in the last 12 months?

## Loan Portfolio (Cortex Analyst)

13. What is the current delinquency rate across our loan portfolio, broken down by loan type?
14. What is the total outstanding loan balance by loan type? What percentage of the portfolio is residential mortgages?
15. Show me all loans with a risk grade of D or E and their collateral coverage (LTV ratio).
16. What is the average DTI ratio for borrowers who are currently delinquent versus those who are current?
17. How many loans are in default status? What is the total exposure amount?
18. What is the weighted average interest rate on our active loan portfolio by rate type (fixed vs variable)?

## Transaction Analytics (Cortex Analyst)

19. What are the top 5 transaction categories by total volume in the last 90 days?
20. How many transactions were flagged as suspicious and what were the most common flag reasons?
21. What is the distribution of transactions by channel (Online Banking, Mobile App, Branch, ATM)?
22. Show me the monthly transaction volume trend for wire transfers over the past year.

## Compliance and Regulatory (Cortex Search + Custom Tools)

23. What are our BSA/AML compliance obligations for correspondent banking relationships?
24. How many SARs have been filed year-to-date and what are the most common event types?
25. What are the current open compliance events at Critical or High severity and who are they assigned to?
26. Explain our CRA performance plan and how we measure community reinvestment.
27. What are the Basel III capital requirements that apply to Cathay Bank?
28. What does our BSA/AML compliance program manual say about transaction monitoring thresholds?

## Risk Management (Custom Tools + Cortex Analyst)

29. Give me the complete risk profile for customer 42 including deposits, loans, compliance events, and risk assessments.
30. What is the overall portfolio summary including total deposits, loans, delinquency rate, and compliance status?
31. How many risk assessments are rated 'High' or 'Critical' and what are the most common risk categories?
32. What is the compliance summary showing all open events, SARs filed, and KYC status across the customer base?

## FIBO Ontology (Custom Tools)

33. What is the FIBO definition of 'Know Your Customer' and what regulatory frameworks require it?
34. How does the ontology connect KYC to AML to BSA? Navigate the relationships.
35. What are the regulatory requirements under the Bank Secrecy Act? What compliance metrics should we track?
36. Resolve the concept 'credit risk' - what data elements does it map to and what are the Basel III requirements?
37. What FIBO relationships exist for 'Anti-Money Laundering'? What concepts does it connect to?

## Customer Support (Cortex Search + Analyst)

38. What are the most common support case categories and what is the average resolution time by category?
39. How many support cases are currently open and what is the breakdown by priority?
40. What is the average customer satisfaction score and how does it vary by support channel?
41. Find support cases related to fraud reports - what patterns do we see?

## Cross-Domain Questions (Multi-Tool)

42. For customers with delinquent loans, what is their average credit score, how many have compliance events, and what are their KYC statuses?
43. Which branches have the highest concentration of high-risk customers and the most compliance events?
44. Compare the profile of customers in the Premier segment versus Standard - deposits, loans, satisfaction scores.
45. Give me a comprehensive risk dashboard: delinquency rates, compliance events by severity, KYC coverage, and average risk scores.
